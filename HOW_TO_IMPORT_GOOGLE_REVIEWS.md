## ‚ùì Common Questions

**Q: Will this post all my reviews publicly?**  
No! Everything imports as **unpublished**. You decide what to share.

**Q: What if I've already reviewed some places in Pachu?**  
The import is smart! It skips restaurants you've already reviewed.

**Q: How long does it take?**  
- Google export: 5-30 minutes (they email you when ready)
- Pachu import: Usually under 30 seconds
- Total: About 30 minutes including Google's processing time

**Q: Can I import reviews multiple times?**  
Yes, but it will only add NEW reviews you haven't shared yet.

**Q: What if something goes wrong?**  
The import gives you a detailed report. If some reviews don't import, it tells you why!

**Q: Will my Google reviews be deleted?**  
Never! Your Google reviews stay exactly as they are. This just makes a copy in Pachu.

## üÜò Need Help?

**Can't find your Google Takeout file?**
- Look in your Downloads folder
- Check your email for the Google Takeout notification
- The file is usually named something like `takeout-20231227...zip`

**Import says "Unrecognized format"?**
- Make sure you selected **JSON** format (not HTML) in Google Takeout
- Make sure you're uploading the right file from inside the ZIP

**Some reviews didn't import?**
- Normal! These are usually:
  - Reviews for places you already reviewed in Pachu
  - Reviews for non-restaurant places (parks, hotels, etc.)
  - Very old reviews that might be missing data
